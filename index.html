Intel's Sustainability Pillars

In 2020, Intel would launch its RISE (Responsible, Inclusive, Sustainable, Enabling) strategy and share its 2030 goals. With this, Intel was aiming to drive industry-wide progress on climate action, water stewardship, and waste reduction sooner rather than later. 2020 was a time of great strife for a lot of people, and yet despite us being physically apart, there was a general sense of togetherness as we all navigated those unprecedented times. It was this spirit of togetherness that Intel hoped to harness not just for the good of the company or the people living in the present, but for the good of the planet and the generations to come that would inherit it.
